R1

Positioning & voice

Firmed May-July 2026. Copy that violates the voice rules must be rewritten before external use.

Never

  • "data center" at top of funnel
  • cutting-edge / revolutionary / seamless / game-changing
  • em dashes, anywhere
  • "total solution" and "modular" as hero words

Say instead

  • purpose-built / proven / configured for
  • AI factory / inference-ready / industrialized
  • own & operate / speed to power
  • express scale in MW / GW, never units or GPUs

Sharpest frame: the industrialized operator - we develop, own & operate, and "the facility is the server." Distilled narrative: Scale, Speed, Change. Moat: construction pedigree.
